Videos

CrashCourse

CrashCourse has a YouTube channel that provides quick and easy to understand videos in every topic in as many subjects as it is humanly possible to teach.

It doesn't have a "mechanics specific playlist of videos, but there are videos in the physics playlist that is useful for mechanics.

Mr Hassaan's Maths Channel

Exactly as the name says, Mr Hassaan's Maths Channel is a YouTube channel that focuses on Mathematics. More specifically, it's a channel that works through questions mostly from past papers and explains them. His channel content is very well arranged.

For M1, he has playlist categories that contain questions from past papers and questions arranged by topic.
